Quick Reference: English Phrases & Their Meanings

Here are 10 of the most common phrases that are a must learn as a beginner.

  • ENGLISH
    ENGLISH
  • Hello
    A greeting
  • How are you?
    A way to ask about someone's well-being
  • Good morning
    A greeting used in the morning
  • Good afternoon
    A greeting used in the afternoon
  • Good night
    A farewell used at night
  • Thank you
    An expression of gratitude
  • Please
    A polite request
  • Where is the bathroom?
    A question asking for the location of the restroom
  • I'm sorry
    An expression of apology
  • See you later
    A farewell indicating you will meet again
What is the past tense of the verb "go"?
Went
What is the meaning of the word "perspicacious"?
Having a keen understanding and insight; able to notice and understand things that are not immediately apparent.
Who is the author of the famous novel "To Kill a Mockingbird"?
Harper Lee
What is the term for a word that has the same spelling but different meanings?
Homograph
What is the term for a word that sounds the same but has different meanings?
Homophone
What is the term for a figure of speech that involves giving human-like qualities to non-human entities?
Personification
What is the term for a sentence that expresses a complete thought?
Independent clause
What is the term for a group of words that functions as a single unit?
Phrase
What is the term for the repetition of initial consonant sounds in words that are close together?
Alliteration
What is the term for the use of words to convey a meaning that is opposite of their literal meaning?
Irony
