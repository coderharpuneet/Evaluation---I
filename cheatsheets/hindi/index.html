Quick Reference: Hindi Alphabet & Common Phrases

Here are 10 of the most common phrases that are a must learn as a beginner.

  • HINDI
    ENGLISH
  • नमस्ते
    Hello
  • आप कैसे हैं?
    How are you?
  • क्या आप अंग्रेज़ी बोलते हैं?
    Do you speak English?
  • यह कितना है?
    How much is this?
  • मुझे समझ नहीं आया
    I didn't understand
  • धन्यवाद
    Thank you
  • कृपया
    Please
  • मेरा नाम [आपका नाम] है
    Where is the bathroom?
  • मुझे माफ़ करें
    I'm sorry
  • मुझे मदद चाहिए
    I need help
What is the Hindi for "Hello"?
नमस्ते (Namaste)
What is the Hindi for "Thank you"?
धन्यवाद (Dhanyavaad)
What is the Hindi for "Goodbye"?
अलविदा (Alvida)
What is the Hindi for "Please"?
कृपया (Kripya)
What is the Hindi for "Good morning"?
शुभ प्रभात (Shubh Prabhat)
What is the Hindi for "Good night"?
शुभ रात्रि (Shubh Raatri)
What is the Hindi for "How are you?"
आप कैसे हैं? (Aap kaise hain?)
What is the Hindi for "I love you"?
मैं तुमसे प्यार करता हूँ (Main tumse pyaar karta hoon)
What is the Hindi for "Excuse me"?
माफ कीजिए (Maaf kijiye)
What is the Hindi for "See you later"?
फिर मिलेंगे (Phir milenge)
