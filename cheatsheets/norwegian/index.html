Quick Reference: Norwegian Alphabet & Common Phrases

Here are 10 of the most common phrases that are a must learn as a beginner.

  • NORSK
    ENGLISH
  • Hei
    Hello
  • Hvordan har du det?
    How are you?
  • God morgen
    Good morning
  • God ettermiddag
    Good afternoon
  • God natt
    Good night
  • Takk
    Thank you
  • Vær så snill
    Please
  • Hvor er toalettet?
    Where is the bathroom?
  • Beklager
    I'm sorry
  • Vi sees senere
    See you later
